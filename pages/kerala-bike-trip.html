Top places in kerala

The distinctive cultures of Kerala lend itself to a number of unique and fascinating attractions. Ride a royal Enfield motorbike on the beautiful and curvy roads of Kerala, to peek into land of cultures, traditions and spices. Varkala to Kappil Beach, Munnar to Annamalai, Munnar Tea Plantations, Munnar to Marayoor, Alleppey to Kuttanad
